Core system:

1. Private access only
    Nobody sees your work unless you approve them. Every client needs an account, verified email/phone, and your permission.
2. Protected gallery
    Before payment: watermark, no download, no share, lower quality preview.
    After payment: 6K/8K full-quality download unlocked.
3. AI photo analyzer
    AI checks every picture before you send it:
    Sharpness
    Lighting
    Face quality
    Composition
    Color grading
    Background
    Emotion
    Overall score

Only photos scoring high go to the client.

4. Screenshot protection
    Use:
    Screenshot blocking where possible
    Screen-record detection
    Dynamic watermark with client name
    Expiring gallery links
    Device/IP verification
    Access logs

Important: nobody can fully stop someone taking a picture with another phone, so watermarking is your strongest protection.

5. Admin control
    You approve:
    Who can see
    Who can download
    Who can share
    Who can screenshot
    When the gallery expires
    Which photos are released
